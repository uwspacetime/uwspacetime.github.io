The Space-Time Reading Group meets weekly to discuss current research in spatiotemporal statistics and related fields.

In Fall 2019, we are reading Spatio-Temporal Statistics with R, by Christopher K. Wikle, Andrew Zammit-Mangion, and Noel Cressie. An online version of the book and associated materials can be found at the book's website.

Meetings are on Wednesday from 12:30pm - 1:30pm in Padelford C-301.. Light refreshment will be provided.

This year, Space-Time Reading Group is jointly organized by John Best and Peter Gao. To receive updates about meetings and other upcoming events, please sign up for the listhost.
