The Space-Time Reading Group at the University of Washington meets weekly to discuss current research in spatiotemporal statistics and related fields.

In past quarters we have focused on themes such as software for fitting spatiotemporal models, using dynamical models to motivate statistical models, and recent research in modeling nonstationary and anisotropic stochastic processes.

We meet during Fall, Winter and Spring Quarters. Light refreshment is provided.

This year, Space-Time Reading Group is jointly organized by John Best and Peter Gao. To receive updates about meetings and other upcoming events, please sign up for the listhost.
